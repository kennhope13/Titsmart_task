# Pham Vi Chuc Nang

## Web/App cho nguoi quan ly

- Dashboard thong ke tien do du an.
- Bieu do phan tram khoi luong cong viec da hoan thanh.
- Tao va quan ly du an dong.
- Import Excel de tao danh sach hang muc cong viec.
- Quan ly va giao viec cho nhan vien hien truong.
- Quan ly vat tu thiet bi.
- Xu ly su co va vuong mac.
- Xuat bao cao Excel/PDF.

## Mobile app cho nhan vien hien truong

- Nhan thong bao.
- Xem danh sach cong viec duoc giao.
- Cap nhat tien do thi cong.
- Xac nhan vat tu.
- Bao cao vuong mac.
- Chup anh hien truong va dinh kem vao cong viec/su co.
